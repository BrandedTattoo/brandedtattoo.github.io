TEX

Hello! I'm Tex, a dedicated tattoo artist with 7 years of professional experience. I began my career in Tucson, AZ, before settling here in beautiful Tennessee.

My passion lies primarily in the powerful aesthetics of Traditional Japanese and the fine detail of Black and Grey tattoos. However, I genuinely enjoy tackling any style or size you bring to the table!

My number one goal is simple: to make sure you are absolutely thrilled with your custom design. We'll take the time to connect, perfectly match our energy, and ensure that whether it's a full dragon or a simple piece, you walk away with a smile!
