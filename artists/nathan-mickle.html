NATHAN MICKLE

Meet Nathan Mickle, a Nashville native tattoo artist with a passion for bold and colorful neo-traditional art and painting. With a unique style that combines classic American traditional influences with contemporary elements, Nathan creates timeless and fresh designs that are highly sought-after in the city's artistic circles. With years of experience and a loyal following, Nathan is known for his ability to translate clients' ideas into stunning custom tattoos. Additionally, his vibrant, intricately designed paintings showcase his talent and unique vision. Nathan is quickly becoming one of Nashville's most renowned artists, leaving a lasting impression on both skin and canvas.
